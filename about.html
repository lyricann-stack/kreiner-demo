Company Profile

Craftsmanship since 1892. Kreiner has been shaping the world of the bathroom for well over a century. What began in Germany more than 130 years ago — founded by George Kreiner and carried forward across five generations — has grown into an international group with a research and innovation center in Germany and manufacturing across Europe and Asia.

Today Kreiner serves brands and distributors across North America, Europe, Australia & New Zealand, India and the Middle East. European design bathtubs are crafted by Kreiner Exklusiv s.r.o. in the Czech Republic; OEM/ODM production runs through our own factory in Thailand and partner facilities in China, with a US assembly plant in Rincon, Georgia entering production in December 2026. Commercial operations run through KREINER (HK) LIMITED.

Our Thai factory holds the certifications North American buyers actually check — cUPC, IAPMO Z124, ASME A112.19.7, ETL — alongside CE and SASO for Europe and the Middle East.

Group Entities

Kreiner Germany — R&D Center

Where it all began in 1892. Our German research and innovation center drives design and product development for the entire group — a complete bathroom portfolio of freestanding bathtubs, whirlpools, aluminium shower screens and steam shower cabins.

Kreiner Exklusiv s.r.o. — Czech Republic

Dedicated manufacturer of exquisite freestanding design bathtubs; sister company of Kreiner s.r.o., serving the Czech market for 25+ years. Production began at Kladno in 2016 and moved to a purpose-rebuilt facility in Horní Bříza, near Plzeň, in 2018. Around 200 models, including Karim Rashid collaborations.
