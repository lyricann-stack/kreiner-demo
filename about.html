Company Profile

Kreiner is a bathtub and shower tray OEM/ODM group serving brands and distributors across North America, Europe, Australia & New Zealand, India and the Middle East. Commercial operations run through KREINER (HK) LIMITED; production runs through our own factory in Thailand and partner facilities in China, with a US assembly plant in Rincon, Georgia entering production in December 2026.

Our Thai factory holds the certifications North American buyers actually check — cUPC, IAPMO Z124, ASME A112.19.7, ETL — alongside CE and SASO for Europe and the Middle East.
